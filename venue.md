## Transports

**Arriving by train**  
To minimise the School’s environmental impact we strongly recommend that you travel to Venice by train whenever feasible. Venice’s main station—Venezia Santa Lucia—is part of Italy’s excellent high speed train network and offers direct trains to many European cities. There are two train companies servicing Venice: [Italo](https://www.italotreno.com/en/destinations-timetable) and [Trenitalia](https://www.trenitalia.com/en.html).

### Transport from Santa Lucia Railway Station
In front of the railway station you find the services for [water transports and the tickets](https://actv.avmspa.it/it) stand. Many hotels can also easily be reached on foot.

**Arriving by plane**

### Transport from Marco Polo airport
*   **Water (direct) and road Taxis (via Piazzale Roma)**: Private water taxi to final destination is expensive (€ 100). Road taxi to Piazzale Roma is ~€ 40.
*   **Alilaguna waterbus service (via city center)**: Final stop at San Marco. Tickets € 15. Visit [Alilaguna](https://www.alilaguna.it/).
*   **ATVO buses (via Piazzale Roma)**: Shuttle buses to Piazzale Roma. Tickets € 10 one way, € 18 return.
*   **ACTV Public bus n°5 (via Piazzale Roma)**: Runs every half hour to Piazzale Roma. Tickets € 10 (€ 18 return).

### Transport from Treviso airport
ATVO buses to Piazzale Roma. The service runs from 7.45 am to 10.30 pm and takes about 50 minutes. € 12 one way, € 22 return.

**Arriving by car**
You will have to pay for a garage/parking space. Last points in Venice: "Piazzale Roma" (Municipal Garage € 24 - € 29 for 24h, Garage San Marco € 30) and "Tronchetto" (€ 21 for 24h). Alternatively, park in front of Mestre train station (€ 5 - € 9 per day) and take a train/bus to Piazzale Roma.



## Moving in Venice

To move in Venice you can use the vaporetto (water bus) service.
Tickets for public transport by boat in the city and the Lagoon are as follows:
*   One-way ticket valid for 75 minutes: € 9.50
*   24 hr - € 25
*   48 hr - € 35
*   72 hr - € 45
*   7 days - € 65

You can buy tickets at offices, machines, or via the [AVM Venezia Official App](https://actv.avmspa.it/it/content/istruzioni-l%E2%80%99uso-avm-venezia-official-app-0). Use [CheBateo](https://chebateo.it/) to plan routes.

**Venezia Unica Card**  
Purchase at Piazzale Roma or Rialto to buy tickets at discounted rates:
*   € 1.50 single ticket
*   € 37 monthly pass
Card cost depends on residency (€ 10 to € 100). Visit [mobility services](https://avm.avmspa.it/en/content/mobility-services).
