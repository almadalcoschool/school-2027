The School will take place at **Venice International University (VIU)**, located on the beautiful island of **San Servolo**, just a 10-minute boat ride from **Piazza San Marco**.

San Servolo is connected to the historic center of Venice by **Vaporetto Line 20**, the public waterbus service. The boat departs from **landing dock B** at **San Zaccaria**, the waterfront adjacent to Piazza San Marco (opposite the Londra Palace Hotel), and provides a direct connection to the island.

For detailed information on how to reach VIU, please visit: [univiu.org/campus-services/how-to-get-to-viu](https://www.univiu.org/campus-services/how-to-get-to-viu)

The timetable for **Vaporetto Line 20** is available here: [Orario Linea 20](https://www.univiu.org/images/aauniviu2017/campus_and_services/Orario_linea_20_dal_2022_04_01.pdf)

All participants and invited speakers will be accommodated at the **San Servolo Residence**, located on the island and within walking distance of the conference venue.

More information about the San Servolo Residential Centre is available here: [Hospitality Services](https://servizimetropolitani.ve.it/en/hospitality)
